Cory Tate is an independent rapper based in Everett, near Seattle, who describes himself as a "professional hobby rapper" and has been active since the mid/late 2000s. He releases his own projects (for example the album Work In Progress and the single Locked Down) and appears as a featured artist on several tracks, notably Space Mountains with Arom, Chris Kemp and Cally Reed on the album Trap In The Cloud produced by PARIGO, as well as Pull Up with Aeon Raps.
