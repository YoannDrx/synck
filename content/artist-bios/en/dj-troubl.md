DJ Troubl’ is a French DJ and turntablist from Poitiers, a two-time DMC World Vice-Champion and multiple French champion. He later revealed himself as the anonymous artist behind the trap project UZ, while continuing to produce music at the crossroads of hip-hop, bass music and EDM.A master of scratching, DJ Troubl’ is equally at ease mixing funk classics, top-shelf US hip-hop and old-school, dancefloor-oriented electro. He is also a world champion specifically in the art of beat juggling.
