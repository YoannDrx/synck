Ducer is an electro-house / EDM producer and artist who has been active since the late 2000s, with several releases to his name, including the EPs Heading (2007), Trafic in the Big City (2008) and the album Cleptomania (2008). He also composes for production music libraries, notably the albums EDM From Paris (Parigo No. 23) and Caught In The Trap (Parigo No. 24), published and produced by PARIGO. His sound draws on EDM, future garage and house influences.
