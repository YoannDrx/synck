Cœur is a French singer and producer associated with the Mutant Ninja scene. Her music blends pop, hip-hop and electronic influences into a dark, introspective aesthetic driven by deeply personal writing.
Charlie Duran, known as Cœur, is a Lyon-born rapper and singer who first emerged as part of the duo Schlaasss. She released her debut mixtape Minouche Mafia in March 2021, produced by Bonetrips and Nicolas Steib. The project mixes rap, electronic music and pop, with poetic, sensual lyrics.
Cœur is also renowned for her singular live performances, where she appears in a white wedding dress surrounded by drag queen dancers. Within LGBT circles in France, she is seen as an important figure of the feminist movement. On stage, she has opened for artists such as Lorenzo, Chilla, Vald, Charlotte Adigéry and Poupie. Most recently, the Lyon-based artist performed at Le Transbordeur in Villeurbanne in December 2023, opening for Troubles.
