Sr Ortegon:
José Miguel Ortegón—better known as “Sr Ortegon”—is an independent producer and composer from Cali, Colombia. Celebrated for his production music, he has been nominated for a Latin Grammy and won a prestigious Mark Award. A distinguished member of the Academy of Television Arts & Sciences (Emmy), he is a key figure in music production worldwide.
For over two decades Sr Ortegon has crafted music for film, television, video games and advertising. His work sits in leading production libraries, cementing his reputation as a go-to composer for picture. With 200+ sync credits, he has shaped major projects such as “Cobra Kai”, “Terminator: Salvation”, “Rambo: Last Blood”, “Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse” (Oscar winner), “Lady Bird” (Oscar nominee), “Mechanic: Resurrection” and the iconic series “The Simpsons”, among others.
He draws inspiration from travels across Europe and Latin America, infusing his music with a distinctive touch and a broad mix of styles. That blend of traditional and modern sounds gives his compositions a richness and originality that stand out in every piece.
His talent and creativity have earned repeated recognition:
- 2017 winner of “Best Production Music Track – World Category” at the Mark Awards (Production Music Association) in Hollywood.
- 2012 “New Talent” honoree at the Cristal Festival in Crans-Montana, Switzerland.
Sr Ortegon continues to leave an indelible mark on production music, bringing innovation and passion to each project and enriching the industry with his distinctive artistic approach.
