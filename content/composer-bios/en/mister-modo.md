Mister Modo is a die-hard crate-digger, beatmaker and seasoned record dealer who has been plying his trade in some of Paris’s most sought-after record shops since the late 1990s. He has released numerous projects, including the celebrated Modonut records with his sidekick Mac Beer. His boundless curiosity, creativity and ever-expanding musical knowledge make him a sharp specialist in “samples & beats”, and he is also an avid collector of rare and seminal records. He has recently released a new album, Music for Bugs.
